O Allah, I intend to perform Umrah, so make it easy for me and accept it from me.

3: Sa'i between Safa and Marwa

During the Sai ritual, pilgrims walk between the hills of Safa and Marwa seven times. They begin by walking from Safa to Marwa for the first round, then return from Marwa to Safa for the second round. This back-and-forth motion is repeated until the seventh round, which concludes at the hill of Marwa. The Sai ritual represents the journey of Hajar (may Allah be pleased with her) as she searched for water and serves as a reminder of faith, determination, and trust in Allah's guidance.

4: Halaq / Qasr
